질의 의도: 제공된 지문(Pecos Bill 이야기)을 바탕으로 **초등 4-5학년 수준의 독해 이해 문제**를 만들기
범위: 객관식 10문제 + True/False 10문제, 문제/답 모두 영어, 마지막에 정답 및 간단 해설 제공

---

핵심 요약

- 이야기의 주요 사건(괴물의 특징, Pecos Bill의 행동, 결말)을 중심으로 문제 구성
- 난이도는 기본 사실 이해 + 간단한 추론 수준
- 보기(A-D)는 균형 있게 분배

---

## Reading Comprehension Quiz

### Part 1: Multiple Choice (10 questions)

1. Who is Pecos Bill?
   A. A farmer
   B. A cowboy
   C. A sailor
   D. A teacher

2. What did the Bear Lake monster look like?
   A. A giant bird
   B. A fish with wings
   C. A snake with tiny legs
   D. A horse with horns

3. What was one thing the monster liked to do?
   A. Sing songs
   B. Hide in caves
   C. Scare swimmers
   D. Fly in the sky

4. Why did the monster flood the shore?
   A. It was raining
   B. It was playing
   C. It was angry and itchy
   D. It was looking for food

5. What did Pecos Bill say he would do?
   A. Run away
   B. Wrestle the monster
   C. Call for help
   D. Build a fence

6. What was the name of Bill’s horse?
   A. Thunder
   B. Widow-maker
   C. Lightning
   D. Storm

7. What happened when Bill got into the lake?
   A. Nothing happened
   B. The water froze
   C. The water became rough
   D. The lake disappeared

8. How did Bill control the monster?
   A. He tied its legs
   B. He used a rope like reins
   C. He shouted loudly
   D. He gave it food

9. Where did the monster land after Bill threw it?
   A. Canada
   B. Mexico
   C. England
   D. Scotland

10. What is the monster called at the end of the story?
    A. Big Snake
    B. Lake Beast
    C. Nessie
    D. Water Dragon

---

### Part 2: True or False (10 questions)

11. Pecos Bill was raised by wolves.
    (True / False)

12. The monster was over 100 miles long.
    (True / False)

13. The monster only ate fish.
    (True / False)

14. Bill enjoyed a good wrestling match.
    (True / False)

15. Bill rode a horse to Bear Lake.
    (True / False)

16. The monster was calm and friendly.
    (True / False)

17. Bill jumped onto the monster’s neck.
    (True / False)

18. The fight made the lake very quiet.
    (True / False)

19. Bill threw the monster across the ocean.
    (True / False)

20. People still say the monster lives in Loch Ness.
    (True / False)

---

## Answers and Explanations

1. B - Pecos Bill is described as a cowboy.

2. C - The monster looked like a snake with tiny legs.

3. C - It liked to scare swimmers.

4. C - It was frustrated and itchy, causing waves.

5. B - Bill said he would wrestle the monster.

6. B - His horse is named Widow-maker.

7. C - The water churned and waves crashed.

8. B - He used a rope like reins to control it.

9. D - It landed in Scotland (Loch Ness).

10. C - It became known as “Nessie.”

11. False - He was raised by coyotes.

12. True - The story says it was over 100 miles long.

13. False - It ate swimmers and even animals.

14. True - Bill said he enjoyed wrestling.

15. True - He rode his horse to the lake.

16. False - The monster was cranky and dangerous.

17. True - He jumped onto its neck.

18. False - The lake became wild and stormy.

19. True - He threw it across the Atlantic Ocean.

20. True - The story says it still lives in Loch Ness.

---

이해도 확인 퀴즈
Q1. Why did Bill use a rope like reins?
Q2. What changed about the monster at the end of the story?

정답 및 해설
A1. To control the monster like riding a horse.
A2. It became shy and was later called Nessie.

FAQ
Q. 이 이야기는 실제 이야기인가요?
A. 아니며, 미국의 과장된 이야기(folktale, tall tale)입니다.

Q. 왜 마지막에 Loch Ness와 연결되나요?
A. 재미있는 상상으로 유명한 “Nessie” 전설과 연결한 것입니다.
